overview:
  title: Arabic safety evaluation across modalities
  paragraphs:
    - >-
      Harmful Arabic content appears in multiple forms: memes that combine
      visual cues with embedded text, and prompts that seek unsafe responses
      from large language models. ArGuard evaluates both settings with a shared
      design that moves from binary detection to fine-grained categorization.
    - >-
      The task emphasizes Arabic-specific challenges such as dialect use,
      sarcasm, code-switching, cultural references, and the interaction between
      language and visual context.
